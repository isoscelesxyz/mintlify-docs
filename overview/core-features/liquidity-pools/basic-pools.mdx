Basic pools use full-range liquidity provision, meaning all deposited tokens are available for trading at every possible price. These pools reward each LP evenly, proportional to their share of deposited assets.

## Benefits for LPs

- **Deposit once**: Low gas fees, no rebalancing or management required
- **Always-on liquidity**: Earn rewards from every trade in the pool
