Aerodrome Finance inherits the contract architecture and full security maintenance from Velodrome V2. As a commitment towards the safety of our users and partners, we maintain transparency about changes and the status of security audits for our smart contracts.

<Warning>
Security audits do not eliminate risks completely. Every user should read and agree to our legal disclaimer before using this protocol.
</Warning>

For security reports, please reach out to us on [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/aerodrome), or to the contacts provided on [our Github page](https://github.com/aerodrome-finance/).

## Bug Bounty Program

Velodrome Finance launched a bug bounty program on June 29, 2023 in collaboration with Immunefi. The Velodrome team's $100,000 bounty was matched by the Optimism Foundation.

<Info>
The program offers up to **$200,000** in bounty for smart contract reports.

[View Bug Bounty Program on Immunefi](https://immunefi.com/bounty/velodromefinance/)
</Info>

### v1 Release

Velodrome v1 launched on **June 2, 2022**. It was reviewed by Code4Rena from **May 23-30, 2022**.

- [Audit Report](https://code4rena.com/audits/2022-05-velodrome-finance-contest)
- [Codebase](https://github.com/velodrome-finance/v1)
- [Contracts](https://github.com/velodrome-finance/v1#contracts)

### v2 and Relay v1 Releases

Velodrome Finance was audited by Spearbit. The audit took place between **February 7-20, 2023**, followed by a two week period for fixes. On **May 22, 2023** a second audit was requested from the same team to finalize any findings after the first round.

The Spearbit audit completed on **June 16, 2023** and the report was published on **July 17, 2023** after being shared with Velodrome v1 forks.

- [Audit Report](https://github.com/spearbit/portfolio/raw/master/pdfs/Velodrome-Spearbit-Security-Review.pdf)
- [Codebase](https://github.com/velodrome-finance/contracts/tree/v2-optimism)
- [Relay Codebase](https://github.com/velodrome-finance/relay)
- [Sugar Codebase](https://github.com/velodrome-finance/sugar)
- [Contracts](https://github.com/velodrome-finance/contracts/tree/v2-optimism#deployment-1)
