This page provides security information for Aerodrome Finance protocol participants, including audit status, emergency governance structure, and verified contract addresses.

## Audits and Bug Bounty

Aerodrome Finance inherits the contract architecture from Velodrome V2, which has undergone comprehensive security audits.

<Info>
For detailed audit reports and the current bug bounty program, visit the [Velodrome security page](https://velodrome.finance/security).
</Info>

**To report security vulnerabilities:**
- Contact the team via [Discord](https://discord.gg/aerodrome)
- Review responsible disclosure guidelines on the [Aerodrome GitHub](https://github.com/aerodrome-finance)

## Emergency Council

<Warning>
The Emergency Council has capabilities that can affect gauges and veNFTs. All council actions are publicly visible on-chain.
</Warning>

To support protocol health during emergency situations, an Emergency Council consists of members from the Aerodrome team and prominent Base community figures. The Council can be activated during emergencies or through governance proposals.

**Council Address:** `0x99249b10593fCa1Ae9DAE6D4819F1A6dae5C013D`

**Council Capabilities:**

- **Set emergency council**: Designate a new emergency council address
- **Gauge management**: <Tooltip tip="Killing a gauge stops emissions; reviving restores them">Kill or revive gauges</Tooltip>
- **Pool customization**: Set custom names or symbols for pools
- **veNFT control**: Activate or deactivate <Tooltip tip="Managed veNFTs - locked positions controlled by strategies">managed veNFTs</Tooltip> (capability transfers to governance when enabled)
