This guide is for protocol teams, token issuers, and project builders on Base. You will learn how to leverage Aerodrome's infrastructure to launch tokens, attract liquidity, and build sustainable growth through aligned incentives.

## Why build with Aerodrome?

Aerodrome Finance provides infrastructure for protocols launching and growing on Base. Whether you're launching a new token or scaling an existing protocol, Aerodrome offers liquidity infrastructure, incentive mechanisms, and direct access to veAERO voters.

### Key advantages

- **Sustainable liquidity**: Access Base's liquidity marketplace through aligned incentive mechanisms
- **Capital-efficient launches**: Fair-launch framework eliminates sniping and reduces launch costs compared to centralized exchange listings
- **Aligned community**: Direct access to veAERO holders who research projects and commit capital
- **Battle-tested infrastructure**: Audited contracts, developer tools, and community support
