<Danger>
  **Using Discord requires caution**

In order to avoid scams and phishing attacks, be wary of unsolicited messages and never share your private keys or seed phrases.

Aerodrome Team members and Discord moderators will **never** request a DM and will never ask you for personal information, your private keys or seed phrases.

</Danger>

## Why join Discord?

The Aerodrome Discord server is the fastest way to:

- **Get technical support** from community members and team
- **Stay updated** on protocol announcements and new features
- **Connect with other users** to share strategies and insights
- **Report bugs** and provide feedback

Visit [guild.xyz/aero](https://guild.xyz/aero) to claim Discord roles and access based on onchain or verified credentials like token or NFT ownership.
